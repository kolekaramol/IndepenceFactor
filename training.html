Course Details

Inner Independence and leadership program
Independent thinking, humility, courage, large-mindedness, positive judgement - are all true marks of a true leader

Friendship with oneself is all important.

Setting up the environment to implant a seed that grows in perfection – freeing from darkness of fear, doubt & ignorance, and culminates into the light of serenity, clarity & knowledge, and to unearth & awaken the inner qualities for leadership, and experience the miracles you can tread on to a broader scale.

Woman and the social integration
When you educate a woman, you educate the entire family, She is the centre of social integration, She makes the home a centre of life, peace and harmony, and improves the quality of life at home and beyond

An educated woman who becomes the wife & mother contributes greatly to family or social development. Woman-soul has the shakti to rebuild the shattered world with a wake up strength of her intuitions, her purity, her simplicity and silent sacrifice. Woman is the architecture of the new generation.

Family Coaching
Companionship, caring & sharing bring real happiness into life, adding to sense of self-worth and self-esteem, a service to family is a gateway to God Kingdom

Let us equip ourselves with the tools that strengthen to resolve to be good, to be better —whether participating individually, with family & friends; give dear ones the best you are capable of! Help one another grow, evolve, and unfold the highest potential. If there is one thing we take for granted in life, apart from the air we breathe in, it is those very relationships that make the life meaningful! We cannot live for ourselves alone.

For Teens and Adolescents
“Good” and “bad” are of your own creation. By imbibing “good” thoughts, you get into touch with “good” forces and you feel happy, and spread the sunshine of joy wherever you go

Participants discover full self-expression to face challenges, invent new possibilities, and feel confident in their own choices. They are empowered to take responsibility of their life & their doings. And they are made able to appreciate the value of physical well being & fitness, and grow in the spirit of compassion & reverence for their families & the people in their life.

Senior citizen and the Life Ahead
Not an end, but a new beginning, time to rediscover the joy of living, make those around you glad of your existence

Don’t retire live in living full.

Whether seventy or sixty, there is in every being’s heart the love for glory and wonder. you are as young as your faith, as old as your doubt, as young as self- confidence as old as fear, as young as your hope, as old as your despair. History records that many people made some of their greatest contributions to society after the age of 65. You are now the honourable senior citizen with so much experience and expertise - exclusive to yourself. Use the tools you possess they may not be earthshaking but with them you can change your world around you.
